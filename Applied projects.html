Power Up Wellbeing! (PUW!)

PUW! is an innovative research project focused on integrating multimodal data to assess student well-being. We focus on cognitively strong students—a diverse group with advanced intellectual abilities, but distinct socio-emotional challenges that are often overlooked by current support systems. By combining physiological, psychological, and socio-economic data, we aim to create a holistic picture of student experiences.

We recently took an important step forward. We’ve successfully validated our multi-modal well-being assessment in a single-case study! This marks a major milestone, proving that our AI-powered methodology can reliably capture stress, well-being, and cognitive functioning in real time. You can read all about it by clicking here.

Our next step is to validate our methodology in a larger sample. We’re currently preparing for a pilot study with 40 students. This study will allow us to further refine our methodology and inform the future of educational support systems. Keep posted for updates!
