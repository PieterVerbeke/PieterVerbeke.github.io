New preprint on human generalization!
https://doi.org/10.31234/osf.io/hgfsq
In our paper, entitled: "Beyond independent latent classes: Testing the limits of human flexibility", we test how efficient humans are in learning about relationships between tasks and using this for generalization.
We test the limits of human flexibility and find that these go well beyond what can be explained by current theoretical frameworks.

About me

I am an AI researcher at Howest University of applied sciences.
I am affiliated to the Cyber3Lab, and the AI Lab.
Here, we try to bridge the gap between the academic and private sector
by implementing the latest cutting-edge research in AI, cybersecurity or immersive technology into your problem.
