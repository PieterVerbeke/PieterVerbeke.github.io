About me

Welcome!
I'm a PhD student at the department of experimental psychology at Ghent University.
In my PhD project, we investigate the function of neural oscillations in human cognitive control. For this purpose, we use computational modelling and EEG. More specifically, we aim to combine these two research methods to explore the limits of model-driven EEG analyses.
All of this is done with intellectual support of Tom Verguts and his research team and financial support of FWO (Research Foundation Flanders)
